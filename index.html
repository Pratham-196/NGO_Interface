What is an NGO?

An NGO is an independent, non-profit group formed by people who come together to work for social, cultural, environmental, or humanitarian causes.

Unlike businesses, NGOs don't aim to make profits; instead, they focus on solving problems in society and helping people or nature.

Featured NGOs

WWF (World Wide Fund for Nature)

WWF works to sustain the natural world for the benefit of people and wildlife, collaborating with partners from local to global levels in nearly 100 countries.

Doctors Without Borders (MSF)

MSF (Doctors Without Borders) provides emergency medical care to people affected by conflicts, epidemics, disasters, and exclusion from healthcare, working with communities in over 70 countries worldwide.

Greenpeace

Greenpeace works to protect the environment and promote peace by addressing global issues such as climate change, deforestation, overfishing, and pollution, campaigning in over 55 countries through nonviolent action and advocacy.

Amnesty International

Amnesty International works to protect and promote human rights worldwide, campaigning against injustice, inequality, and abuses through research, advocacy, and mobilizing people in over 150 countries.

CARE International

CARE International works to fight global poverty and social injustice, delivering humanitarian aid and development programs with a focus on empowering women and girls in over 100 countries.

International Red Cross

The International Red Cross works to protect human life and health, provide emergency assistance, disaster relief, and education, operating worldwide guided by principles of neutrality, impartiality, and humanity.

Save the Children

Save the Children works to improve the lives of children worldwide by providing healthcare, education, protection, and emergency aid, reaching millions of children in over 100 countries.

CRY (Child Rights and You)

CRY works to ensure underprivileged children in India have access to education, healthcare, protection from exploitation, and a happy, safe childhood by partnering with local communities and organizations.

Smile Foundation

Smile Foundation works to empower underprivileged children, youth, and women in India through education, healthcare, livelihood, and women empowerment programs, reaching disadvantaged communities across the country.

GiveIndia

GiveIndia works to alleviate poverty in India by enabling individuals and organizations to donate transparently to verified nonprofits, supporting causes like education, healthcare, livelihood, and disaster relief.

Akshaya Patra Foundation

The Akshaya Patra Foundation works to eliminate classroom hunger in India by implementing the Mid-Day Meal Programme, providing nutritious meals to millions of school children to support education and health.

Pratham

Pratham works to improve the quality of education in India by providing learning support to children, conducting large-scale literacy and numeracy programs, and driving innovative education initiatives across the country.

HelpAge India

HelpAge India works to support the elderly by providing healthcare, livelihood, and advocacy services, aiming to ensure dignity, protection, and active participation of senior citizens across the country.

War Child

War Child works to protect, educate, and empower children affected by armed conflicts, providing psychological support, education, and safe environments in war-affected regions worldwide.

PATH

PATH works to improve health and well-being worldwide by developing and delivering innovative solutions in vaccines, medicines, diagnostics, and health programs, particularly for underserved communities.

Oxfam

Oxfam works to fight global poverty, inequality, and injustice by providing humanitarian aid, promoting sustainable development, and advocating for social and economic change in over 90 countries.

World Vision

World Vision works to improve the lives of children, families, and communities worldwide by providing education, healthcare, nutrition, and emergency relief, while promoting long-term sustainable development.

Teach For India

Teach For India works to provide quality education to children from underprivileged communities by recruiting and training fellows to teach in low-income schools and drive systemic change in education.

Goonj

Goonj works to address basic needs and promote sustainable development in India by recycling urban waste into usable resources, running clothing and sanitation drives, and empowering rural communities.

Blue Cross of India

Blue Cross of India works to prevent cruelty to animals and promote their welfare in India through rescue, rehabilitation, adoption programs, and awareness campaigns.

UNHCR (UN Refugee Agency)

UNHCR (United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees) works to protect and support refugees, asylum-seekers, and displaced people worldwide, providing shelter, food, healthcare, and advocating for their rights and durable solutions.
