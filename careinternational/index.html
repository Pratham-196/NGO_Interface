Our Mission

CARE International is a global leader in fighting global poverty and providing humanitarian assistance. We work in over 100 countries, reaching more than 90 million people through over 1,000 poverty-fighting development and humanitarian aid projects. Our mission is to save lives, defeat poverty, and achieve social justice.

Our Values

üíô Respect

We affirm the dignity, potential, and contribution of participants, donors, partners, and staff. We value diversity and embrace inclusion.

ü§ù Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of integrity, accountability, and transparency in all our actions and relationships.

üåç Commitment

We work alongside the communities we serve, focusing on long-term solutions and sustainable development.

üë• Excellence

We challenge ourselves to the highest levels of learning and performance, achieving impact through innovation and evidence-based solutions.

Our Core Programs

üë© Women & Girls Empowerment

We believe that empowering women and girls is one of the most effective ways to fight poverty. Our programs focus on education, economic opportunity, health, and leadership development for women and girls worldwide.

üè• Health & Nutrition

We work to improve access to quality healthcare, reduce maternal and child mortality, combat malnutrition, and strengthen health systems in the communities we serve.

üåæ Food Security

We help communities build resilient food systems, improve agricultural practices, and ensure access to nutritious food for all, especially during times of crisis.

üíº Economic Opportunity

We create pathways to economic self-reliance through microfinance, savings groups, vocational training, and support for small businesses, especially for women.

üö® Emergency Response

We provide life-saving assistance during humanitarian crises, including food, water, shelter, and protection services, while helping communities rebuild and recover.

üè´ Education

We work to ensure all children, especially girls, have access to quality education. We support schools, provide scholarships, and work to remove barriers to education.

FY2023 Impact Snapshot

CARE reached 174 million people across 102 countries

Guided by women-led solutions, CARE responded to 54 humanitarian crises and supported 560+ long-term development projects in 2023. The majority of participants were women and girls, reflecting our focus on gender equality and resilience.

Women & Girls 70% of total participants
Cash & Voucher Aid $715M delivered directly to families
Food Security 32M people gained reliable food access
Health Services 31M received essential care
Education & Skills 12M learners supported
Climate Adaptation 14M people building resilience
