Defending Our Planet for a Green and Peaceful Future

For over 50 years, Greenpeace has used non-violent, creative action to pave the way towards a greener, more peaceful world, and to confront the systems that threaten our environment.

Our Mission

Greenpeace is an independent campaigning organisation, which uses peaceful, direct action to expose global environmental problems and develop solutions for a green and peaceful future. Our goal is to ensure the ability of the earth to nurture life in all its diversity.

Our Core Campaigns

üå≥ Protect Forests

We work to end deforestation and protect our planet's vital forests, which are crucial for biodiversity and fighting climate change. Our campaigns target illegal logging, palm oil expansion, and forest fires.

üåä Save the Oceans

From ending plastic pollution to stopping destructive fishing, we campaign for a network of ocean sanctuaries to protect marine life. We fight against overfishing, deep-sea mining, and ocean acidification.

‚òÄÔ∏è Climate Action

We demand a shift from fossil fuels to renewable energy sources to tackle the climate crisis. Our campaigns target coal plants, oil drilling, and promote clean energy solutions worldwide.

‚ôªÔ∏è Live Sustainably

We challenge corporations to reduce their environmental impact and promote solutions that help people live more sustainably. We campaign for sustainable agriculture, clean transportation, and waste reduction.

üõ°Ô∏è Protect Biodiversity

We work to protect endangered species and their habitats, campaign against wildlife trafficking, and promote conservation efforts that preserve the planet's incredible biodiversity.

‚ö° Clean Energy

We campaign for a rapid transition to 100% renewable energy, oppose nuclear power, and promote energy efficiency solutions that reduce our carbon footprint and create green jobs.
